Our Story

  • Owners Wanda and Shelton Fly opened Burger Barn in 1979 soon after having their first child. There were originally several locations but they eventually closed the others and put everything they had into the main location on Lexington Avenue, which is where they are still located today. There have been many businesses around Burger Barn that have come and gone but they wanted to stay somewhere that felt like home.

Owner

  • Wanda Fly's husband Shelton Fly passed away many years ago and now she runs the business on her own. She is a Jackson native and truly adores how her city has blossomed in her lifetime. She now has three children and many grandchildren. She attends Lighthouse Church here in Jackson and plays a big part in the church as well.

Workers

  • One of the best parts about Burger Barn is that the employees work as one big family. Regina, Fred, Kay, and Jennifer have all been working with Wanda over twenty years, while Robert and Kim have been there for over five years. This is a big accomplishment in the food industry because employee turnover is very frequent.
