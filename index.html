Welcome to the Barn

If you're in Jackson and are searching for that perfect burger, then you have to come to our convenient location! Burger Barn is a nice local restaurant in the downtown area that serves burgers, sandwiches, sides, desserts, and so much more! All of our food is made right on the spot as soon as you order.
